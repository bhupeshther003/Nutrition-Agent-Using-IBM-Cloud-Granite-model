COMPANY LEAVE POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per year. Annual leave accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per month. Employees must submit leave requests at least 2 weeks in advance for approval by their manager.

2. SICK LEAVE
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days. Unused sick leave does not carry over to the next year.

3. REMOTE WORK POLICY
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval. Remote work requests must be submitted 24 hours in advance. Employees must be available during core business hours (10 AM - 4 PM) and maintain productivity standards.

4. TRAVEL POLICY
Business travel must be pre-approved by the department head. Employees should book economy class for flights under 4 hours. Hotel accommodation should not exceed $200 per night. All expenses must be submitted within 30 days with receipts.

5. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Employees can claim reimbursement for business-related expenses including meals (up to $50 per day), transportation, and client entertainment. All claims must be submitted through the expense management system with valid receipts within 30 days of the expense date.

6. WORKING HOURS
Standard working hours are 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday. Employees are entitled to a 1-hour lunch break. Overtime work requires prior approval and is compensated at 1.5x the regular hourly rate.

7. SECURITY POLICY
All employees must use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication. Confidential company data must not be shared externally without authorization. Employees must report any security incidents immediately to the IT department.

8. CODE OF CONDUCT
Employees are expected to maintain professional behavior at all times. Harassment, discrimination, or any form of misconduct will not be tolerated. Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

For questions about these policies, please contact the HR department at hr@company.com or extension 1234.
